Caetano Veloso

an aging chameleon

These are the main events in Caetano's life:

  • 1942 - Born in Santo Amaro, Bahia, Brazil
  • 1959 - Hears for the first time the song Chega de Saudade, by Bossa Nova's father João Gilberto. He later recalled: "It was the clearest landmark a song
    has ever left in my life"
  • 1963 - First musical work. After impressing the director Álvaro Guimarães with a speech on how João Gilberto revitalized Samba, Caetano is invited to compose
    the soundtrack to the plays Boca de Ouro, by Nelson Rodrigues, and A Exceção e a Regra, by Bertold Bretch.
  • 1965 - Moves to Rio de Janeiro with his sister Maria Betânia
  • 1967 - Releases, in a partnership with Gal Costa, his first album Domingo.
  • 1967 - Releases his first self-titled solo album, giving birth to the Tropicalist movement, which would change the history of Brazilian music.
  • 1968 - Caetano performs the provocative song É Proibido Proibir with Os Mutantes at the 3ª International Song Festival to a booing crowd. Caetano then improvises
    an angry, and now historical, speech against the judges and the public.
  • 1968 - Because of his political views, Caetano is sent to jail by the Military Regime.
  • 1969 - Caetano moves to England with his friend Gilberto Gil, where he lives in exile until 1972.
  • 1971 - Releases one of his best albums, the acclaimed Transa, which contained covers and songs with English subtitles.
  • 1972 - Returns to Brazil and performs with João Gilberto and Gal Costa at TV Tupi.
  • 1980 - Caetano starts growing in popularity abroad, especially in Portugal, Israel, France and Africa.
  • 1983 - Meets Paula Lavigne, who was 14 years old. She would later become his long-time wife, with whom Caetano had 2 kids.
  • 1990 - Participates in the Montreaux Jazz Festival in Switzerland.
  • 1991 - Writes a profound article to the New York Times about the cultural implications of Carmem Miranda
  • 2002 - Launches a book about the tropicalia movement, Tropical Truth: A Story of Music and Revolution in Brazil.
  • 2010 - The artist becomes a constant meme object on the internet.
  • 2016 - Caetano performs at the Rio Olimpic Games with Anitta and long-time friend Gilberto Gil.
  • 2020 - Wins a millionaire judicial dispute against Olavo de Carvalho, the intellectual mentor of the Bolsonaro family
